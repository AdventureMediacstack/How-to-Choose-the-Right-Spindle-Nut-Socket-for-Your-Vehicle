FAQ Section

Q1: What size do I need for my Toyota?
Most Toyota trucks and SUVs require a 54mm socket, making it the standard size for these applications.

Q2: Can I use a regular socket instead?
No. Regular sockets are often too shallow or thin-walled, which can lead to stripped nuts. Using the correct specialized tool ensures a snug, secure fit.

Q3: How do you remove a nut without the right tool?
It’s not recommended. Using pliers or makeshift tools risks damaging the nut and axle threads. Always use the proper tool designed for this job.

Q4: Are these sockets universal?
No. Sizes vary by vehicle. Toyota trucks and SUVs commonly use 54mm, while other makes and models may require different sizes.

Q5: Is it impact-rated?
Yes. The socket is made from hardened steel and can handle impact tools, providing durability for demanding repair tasks.
